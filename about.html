About Me

Yes- Hello, Heyo! They call me Jin My name's Jin Fitz. I like drawing, music, photography, video making, and more. I created this portfolio site in my sophomore year of college using Github and PheonixCode.

I was born in Chongqing China, and was adopted and taken to the United States when I was just a 2 year old baby. As a senior in high school, I was apart of the e-Communication, 21st century academy for Entertainment Video Production. I am currently in college working towards my Bachelors in Science - Digital Media; Visual Imaging. I think doing illustration, graphic design or commercial videography / photography in my future would be what I'd like to do, as well as for-fun content making on social media as a hobby, and perhaps even starting my own small business with my art work.â€‹

Along with digital media, I've always loved music. I have a small collection of instruments that I keep. I've played violin since 5th grade, and have always had an interest in other instruments like guitar, ukulele, and kalimba. Seeing old worn and used instruments, thinking of the music they once made. The passion that can come from one's music through lyric or sound is truly inspiring.
I've been exposed to the world of photography since I was young, a family friend was doing photography with my mother has a hobby, so I grew up with the exposure to photo and video taking. Sometime in high school, I got really into the art of photo taking and editing, and realized my love for using cameras. Taking film photos and scanning them myself is also a fun way for me to switch up my photography and make it into something special and different. I have a small collection of film cameras I randomly find from various thrift shops I find while I travel, and find interest in trying them out and seeing how they compare to other cameras, and how the different film looks.

One day I'd like to travel around and see what the world has to offer. Some bucket list places are most certainly, China, Canada, Japan, Italy, Brighton, Ireland, and many more! I've now been abroad to Portugal and will be traveling to Ireland in Spring 2025 for a short study abroad trip in Dublin. I'm so excited to learn about a new perspective to business and culture, and meeting new people from outside of the United States is always a fun, eye opening experience!

What I think truly sparked my passion for art and creativity was when my cousin showed me this game on my iPad mini, called Minecraft. My love for Minecraft then introduced me into the world of artwork, youtubers, video games, and showed me this new world filled with passionate people who just love making and creating different things!
